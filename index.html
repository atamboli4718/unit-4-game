Crystal Collector

Instructions

Welcome to Crystal Collector! Below you will find 4 crystals, each with a random hidden value between 1-12. The target number is a randomly generated number. Your job is to click on the crystals to add the numbers together until they calculate to the target number. If you go over the target number you loose and the game is over. Try to rackup as many wins as you can!
